Each week you have an allocated $75 to spend on wellness. This means Gym Memberships, massages, and most things that promote a healthy lifestyle. If you have questions about whether your preferred activity qualifies for wellness reimbursement, please contact Emily.

How do I Fully Lunch?

Every Thursday is Fully Lunch day. That means lunch is on us so long as you aren't working from home! Venture card purchases for lunch need to be followed up by filling out the proper form. If you have any questions, please direct them to our resident lunch enthusiast Nate.
